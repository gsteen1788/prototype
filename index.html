Common Questions

How does Value Wallet work? expand_more
Your Value Wallet grows every month you stay protected without making a claim. We reward you for community participation, maintaining your safety streak, and helping your circle grow. You can withdraw your earned value at any time.
How do I make a claim? expand_more
Tap the relevant cover on your Protect screen, then select "Start a Claim." You'll need a photo and brief description. Most claims are processed within 48 hours. For urgent claims, use our WhatsApp line for instant assistance.
Can I pause my cover? expand_more
Yes! You can pause any cover for up to 3 months. During this time your premiums are reduced but you won't be covered for new events. Your Value Wallet balance is preserved. Go to the home screen and tap "Pause Cover."
What is Community Pool? expand_more
The Community Pool is a shared safety net. When your circle stays healthy (no claims), the pool grows and everyone earns more. If a member needs to claim, the pool supports them — that's the power of community protection.
How is Weather Reset triggered? expand_more
Weather Reset uses parametric insurance — payouts are triggered automatically when official weather station data shows rainfall fell below or exceeded the seasonal threshold for your area. No claim needed. Payments arrive within 72 hours of the weather event.
